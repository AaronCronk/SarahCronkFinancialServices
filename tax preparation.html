Sarah Cronk Financial Services - Tax Preparation


Indvidual Taxes

As a CPA, I am qualified to prepare all types of federal and state tax returns for individuals, corporations, and nonprofit organizations. Additionally, I work closely with all of my clients to develop a long-term tax strategy with the objective of reducing the amount of taxes which they are legally obligated to pay. I receive annual training and education on the changes to the tax code to ensure that my clients receive the most up-to-date guidance. It's not too late to get started!


Quarterly & Sales Tax Filings

Did you know that if you have operated a small business for more than one year, the government requires you to file quarterly estimates of your tax burden. Additionally, the State of Iowa and certain municipalities require you to collect and file sales tax on any retail sales. If your business fails to file these taxes, you could be subject to substantial interest and penalties on the amount owed. I can help you calculate and file these crucial tax returns.
